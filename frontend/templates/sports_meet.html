Annual Sports Meet

Join us for an exciting day of sports, teamwork, and fun!

Events

Track and field, football, basketball, relay, tug of war and many more events will be conducted across different categories.

All registered students are welcome to participate. You can register for a maximum of three events.

How to register?

Contact your class coordinator or visit the department office to fill out the registration form before the deadline.

For any queries, please reach out to the Sports Committee at sports@sriher.edu.in.
